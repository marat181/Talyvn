How It Works

How the Talyvn Desktop Time Tracking App Works

Simple, Transparent & Automatic

The Talyvn desktop time tracking app is designed to be simple, transparent, and automatic. After logging in, users start tracking by selecting a project or task and pressing the play button. Once tracking begins, activity signals are captured automatically in the background, without interrupting the workflow.

Start Tracking Screenshot

Accurate Time, No Guesswork

To ensure accurate time logs, the app automatically shuts down when the user is idle, preventing inactive time from being recorded. All settings are fully customizable and flexible, allowing companies to configure the app to match their preferred structure, workflows, and internal policies.

Idle Detection Screenshot

Dashboards & Reports in Real Time

All tracked data syncs in real time to dashboards and reports, giving employees full visibility into their time logs and activity history, while managers access aggregated, permission-based insights for accurate, structured workforce oversight.
