Overview

What is Leave Management & Why Does it Matter

Leave Management is the process of tracking, approving, and handling employee time off requests.

A modern leave management system helps HR teams with:

→ tracking PTO, sick leave, & unpaid time off
→ approving or rejecting leave requests through structured workflows
→ maintaining accurate leave balances for employees
→ ensuring proper workforce coverage

Talyvn offers all these essential benefits & more to simplify this process while connecting leave data with attendance tracking, scheduling, and reporting.

Approval Workflow

Approve Leave Faster & Save HR Time

Manual approval chains usually slow the entire leave management process. To fix that, our HR leave management system offers structured approval workflows.

01
Eligibility Check
Automated validation

02

Configurable layers
