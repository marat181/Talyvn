Overview

What is Payroll Software?

In simple terms, Payroll Software is a system that helps organizations automatically manage, calculate, and distribute salaries for their workforce.

Instead of manually calculating total hours worked by an employee, overtime, bonuses, taxes, deductions, and final salary payout, payroll processing software handles all these tasks for you automatically.

Modern payroll software systems like the Talyvn go even further by integrating payroll with

⏱time tracking
✓payroll approvals
📊reporting and analytics
💸payout execution
👥workforce management

When an advanced system like Talyvn combines all these essential elements, it makes payroll faster, more accurate, and far easier to manage for companies.

How It Works

Calculate Payroll in Seconds with Talyvn

Talyvn simplifies payroll processing into just 3 easy steps. There is no need for complicated spreadsheets or endless calculations when you complete the process with the help of our HR payroll solutions.

01
Step 1: Preview the Payroll

Select a date range for payroll calculations, then filter the results by employees or teams. Once the filters are applied, you will be able to

💵edit employee hourly rates
🎁add bonuses or penalties
📉apply deductions
👁preview net salary in both USD & payout currency

Everything updates in real time.

02
Step 2: Review and Approve

Once you are done with the preview and ensure the payroll looks correct, it will be saved as a new cycle and moved to the Review & Approve section.

If you approve the payroll at this stage, the system will automatically lock the amounts and exchange rates to ensure financial accuracy.

03
Step 3: Finalize Payroll

Once you preview and approve the payroll, it will be ready for an actual payout.

Talyvn supports Web3-based payroll processing, enabling companies to complete payouts directly from their own wallets. During this step, transactions will be grouped by network, and you can monitor them until they complete.
