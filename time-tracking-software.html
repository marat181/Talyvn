Overview

What is Talyvn Time Tracking Software?

Talyvn is a system that turns raw work hours into structured, and useful workforce data.

Instead of simply recording hours, the platform helps companies to understand

→ How work time is spent
→ How projects use team capacity
→ Where productivity improves or slows down

While many tracking tools only record clock-in/clock-out times, Talyvn goes further by combining

→ Activity tracking
→ Project allocation
→ Role-based approvals
→ Detailed reporting

This helps organizations manage productivity, projects, and payroll more accurately.

How It Works

How Talyvn Workforce Time Tracking Software Works?

01
Intelligent, Activity-Based Tracking
Structured activity signals across devices without constant supervision

Once tracking starts, Talyvn records structured activity signals across the devices that your team uses.

Without constant supervision, the system helps companies understand work patterns.

Key capabilities include

⚡Automated idle time detection
⚙️Configurable tracking metrics
📅Exclusion of meeting time from inactivity calculations
💡This feature helps managers gain reliable productivity insights while still allowing employees to retain control over their workflow.
02
Structured Time Entry Controls
Clean data starts with clear tracking rules

Clean data starts with clear tracking rules.

Talyvn allows admins to configure how employees log work hours.

With Talyvn, admins can

👥Designate project managers to view/edit/delete tracking data independent of team hierarchy
✏️Configure whether manual time entries are allowed or not
🏷️Enforce client, project, task, and tag selection before the tracking begins
💲Assign billable rates per project or per employee
💡Structured entries prevent messy timesheets and make reporting, billing, and payroll far more accurate.
03
Adjustments and Time Splitting
Support corrections while preserving valuable data

Mistakes happen during time tracking.

Therefore, Talyvn is designed to support corrections while preserving valuable data.

With Talyvn work hours tracking software, admins can

✂️Adjust tracked time by splitting a single session into multiple parts
🗑️Delete invalid entries with confirmation
➕Add manual time to employee timesheets
🔀Split time across different projects, clients, and tasks
💡Accurate adjustments ensure project billing and payroll calculations stay correct.
04
Real-Time Reporting & Synchronization
All tracked data syncs instantly with company dashboards

While using the Talyvn team time tracking software, all tracked data from activities to time entries instantly syncs with the company dashboards.

Talyvn's customizable reporting features include:

📝Detailed employee timesheets including tracked hours & breaks
📁Project hours
👤Client totals
⏰Overtime tracking
💰Billable vs. non-billable breakdowns
🏖️PTO integration
💡Real-time visibility helps companies make faster operational decisions and prevents billing errors.

Compliance

Key Aspects of the Ethical Employee Monitoring

Talyvn is designed to provide activity monitoring and time tracking services in compliance-sensitive environments through:

🔍
Complete Audit Trails
Every change, adjustment, deletion, and screenshot action is logged in a precise time tracking audit trail. At the Talyvn app, when deleting or editing workforce data, admins must provide a valid reason, and employees are notified when those changes are made.
🔐
Role-based Permissions
Talyvn's customizable solutions enable role-based permissions to protect data integrity while ensuring accountability. While product owners & admins have full access, managers will have team-level control, and employees will be able to view or edit only their own entries.
📋
Transparent Screenshot and Time Policies
In the Talyvn app, users can delete screenshots, request their deletion, and receive notifications when management edits their entries. The balance of oversight and full transparency makes Talyvn the best time tracking software for regulated industries.
