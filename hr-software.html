Overview

What Is HR Software?

HR software represents a type of tool that can simplify all types of people operations processes in a single centralised platform. Optimizing all the HR related activities and automating various routine cycles allows the human resources team to focus on what matters most — building a company culture, streamlining employee communications, and focusing on productivity growth.

Talyvn is one of the emerging workforce management software solutions with an all-in-one approach. The platform incorporates a traditional HRIS software system with:

→ Centralized data management
→ Payroll and client billing software
→ Performance management & HR analytics
→ Project management tools for HR and finance
