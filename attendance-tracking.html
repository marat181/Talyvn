How the Talyvn Attendance Monitoring System Works

Talyvn's employee attendance management system operates smoothly by connecting three core layers: Scheduling & Shifts, Time Tracking Activity, and Automated Classification.

When the employee shift is assigned, the system automatically compares the planned schedule with the actual activity data. Then, Talyvn time attendance software classifies the results as:

In the admin dashboard, organizations can customize these metrics according to their company's policies and requirements. With this online attendance tracking solution, there is no need for manual reviews as it is based on real-time data.

Built-in Configuration & Policy Controls

As a customizable attendance tracking solution, Talyvn enables companies to define grace periods for delays, enable or disable specific statuses, set minimum shift duration requirements, and trigger automatic notifications for repeated missed shifts. For companies that need policy alignment, Talyvn offers flexible solutions and configurable settings that they can utilize for employee attendance tracking.

Define Grace PeriodsSet how many minutes of delay are acceptable before the system flags an employee as late.
Enable / Disable StatusesTurn specific attendance statuses on or off to match your internal classification policy.
Minimum Shift DurationSet requirements for how long a shift must be tracked before it counts as completed.
Auto Alerts for Repeated Missed ShiftsAutomatically trigger notifications when an employee repeatedly misses scheduled shifts.
